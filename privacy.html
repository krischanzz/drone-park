Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

Drone Park respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have when using the Drone Park mobile application and related website.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Drone Park may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and password, when you register or sign in.
  • Profile information, such as your nickname, which may be visible to other users.
  • User-generated content, including spots you add, reviews, ratings, bookmarks, and reports you submit.
  • Location information, if you grant permission, to help show your position on the map and improve the map experience.
  • Technical and usage data, such as app interactions or diagnostics, to maintain and improve the service.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To display map content and help users discover drone spots.
  • To save bookmarks, reviews, ratings, and contributed locations.
  • To help maintain safety, moderation, and abuse prevention features.
  • To improve the app, troubleshoot issues, and support users.

Location Data

If you allow location access, Drone Park may use your device location while the app is in use to show nearby areas and help with map browsing. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings. Drone Park integrates with mapping services such as Apple Maps and may open external navigation apps like Google Maps. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

How Information Is Stored

Drone Park uses Firebase (a service provided by Google) for authentication, database storage, and backend functionality. Firebase may collect technical data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Account Deletion

If you delete your account, all associated data including your profile, reviews, and bookmarks will be permanently removed. We keep the rights to keep the created spots.

Sharing of Information

Drone Park does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to:

  • Provide the app’s core functionality.
  • Work with trusted service providers that help operate the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect the rights, safety, and integrity of users or the service.

User Content Visibility

Content you choose to contribute, such as nicknames, spot reviews, ratings, and created spots, may be visible to other users inside the app.

Data Retention

Information is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies. If you delete your account, associated content may be removed in accordance with how the app is designed and operated.

Your Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may change device permissions, including location access, in system settings.
  • You may request account deletion through the app’s available account controls.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system can be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Drone Park is not intended for children under 13, and the service is not knowingly directed to them.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access – You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification – You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure – You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing – You can request limited use of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability – You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object – You can object to how your data is processed.

You can exercise these rights at any time by contacting us.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract – To provide the app’s functionality (account, features).
  • Legitimate Interest – To improve the app and maintain functionality.
  • Consent – For optional features such as location access.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
